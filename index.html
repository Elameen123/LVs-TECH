Project Overview

Welcome to the Smart Water Meter Project website! Our project aims to revolutionize water usage monitoring and promote water clarity awareness. By utilizing advanced sensors and technology, we provide precise measurements of water volume and clarity in real-time.

Our smart water meter system consists of several key components:

  • A flow sensor: This sensor accurately measures the volume of water passing through the meter, enabling accurate monitoring of water consumption.
  • turbidity sensor: This sensor assesses the clarity of the water, providing insights into its quality and potential impurities.
  • LED indicators: We have incorporated three light emitting diodes (LEDs) that display the clarity status of the water. The LEDs indicate whether the water is clear, slightly unclear, or not clear, offering a visual representation of water quality.
  • A GSM SIM800L module: Our smart water meter sends the recorded water consumption data through the GSM module, enabling remote access to usage information and facilitating real-time monitoring.
  • A Micro SD card reader: All the collected data is stored securely on a Micro SD card, ensuring data backup and enabling offline access to historical usage records.

Through our website, you can explore real-time data visualizations, gain insights into water usage patterns, and monitor water clarity trends. Stay informed and take control of your water consumption for a more sustainable future!

Case Study And Project

Water is a precious resources, and its efficient management is becoming increasingly important in today's world. Traditional water metering systems often fail to provide real-time data on water usage and water quality is poor. To remove these limitations, LVs Tech Engineerig Company aims to design and implement a comprehensive system that not only measures water flow but also verifies its transparency. The main goal of this project is to enable individuals to effectively monitor and manage their water consumption. Using an Arduino Uno micro controller in combination with a flow sensor, the system accurately measures flow in real-time. This data gives individuals insight into their water use patterns, identifies potential protected areas, and helps them make informed decisions about their water use. The project includes a turbidity sensor for checking water clarity. However, this informatio can be stored using a Micro SD Card Reader. This feature allows users to assess the quality of the water they consume, ensure safety and increase awareness of water health. By integrating the SIM800L GSM module into the system, the project enables remote monitoring and data transmission. This feature not only increases comfort, but also enables efficient water management at scale. The collected data can be analyzed and used for various purposes such as water resource planning, infrastructure maintenance and conservation efforts. Overall, the smart water meter project aims to help individuals make informed decisions about their water consumption and contribute to broader water management efforts.
